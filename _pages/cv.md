## Current Position

**Postdoctoral Researcher**
[Department of Applied Mathematics and Computer Science (DTU Compute)](https://www.compute.dtu.dk/), Cognitive Systems Section
Technical University of Denmark (DTU) · Copenhagen, Denmark
*2023 – Present*

Working on the [**Reading the Reader (RtR)**](https://datascience.novonordiskfonden.dk/projects/reading-the-reader/) project funded by the **Novo Nordisk Foundation** (grant NF22OC0076877), in collaboration with Associate Professor [Per Bækgaard](https://people.compute.dtu.dk/pgba/) and the Royal Danish Academy – Architecture, Design, Conservation. Research combines eye-tracking, pupillometry, Bayesian modelling, and adaptive AI interfaces to understand and support individual reading patterns.

## Previous Positions

**Postdoctoral Researcher**
[Affective Intelligence and Robotics Laboratory (AFAR)](https://cambridge-afar.github.io/), Department of Computer Science and Technology
University of Cambridge, UK
*2021 – 2023*

Contributed to the [**WorkingAge**](https://www.workingage.eu/) project (**EU Horizon 2020**, grant No. 826232). Developed affective computing systems for inferring workers' emotional states using facial analysis, dimensional affect modelling (valence/arousal), and multimodal sensing in naturalistic work-like environments.
